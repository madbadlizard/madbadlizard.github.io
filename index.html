About

I make performance in this bloody world.
I dance but I am not a dancer. I perform but I am not an actor. I was doing literature and I am good at broken language.
I am fascinated at boundaries.
I chase myself to the edge and see what happens.
I create for transformation not for understanding. People should stop understanding others but feeling others, that's the only way the world could be better.
